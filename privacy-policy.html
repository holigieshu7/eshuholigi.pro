1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Eshuholigi.pro ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") collects, uses, processes, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services. This Policy is governed by the Information Technology Act, 2000 ("IT Act"), the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011 ("SPDI Rules"), the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, and other applicable Indian laws.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Demographic information such as age, gender, preferences
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment gateways)
  • Technical information including IP address, browser type, device information
  • Usage data and website analytics

2.2 Sensitive Personal Data

As defined under the SPDI Rules, we may collect sensitive personal data including:

  • Financial information (bank account, credit card details via secure payment gateways)
  • Biometric information (if applicable to our services)
  • Any other information deemed sensitive under applicable laws

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To process transactions and payments
  • To communicate with you about our services
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • To prevent fraud and ensure security
  • For marketing purposes (with your consent)

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on:

  • Your explicit consent
  • Performance of a contract with you
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Legitimate business interests

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers and business partners (under strict confidentiality)
  • Law enforcement agencies when required by law
  • Regulatory authorities as mandated by Indian laws
  • Third parties with your explicit consent

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security practices and procedures as required under the IT Act and SPDI Rules, including:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security audits and assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Incident response procedures

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

Personal data will be securely deleted or anonymized when no longer required.

Under Indian privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information

  • Withdraw consent (where processing is based on consent)
